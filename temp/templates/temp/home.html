PU-COURTMATE

Welcome to PU-COURTMATE, your premier destination for hassle-free badminton court booking at Pondicherry University. Developed under the auspices of the Physical Education Department, PU-COURTMATE aims to revolutionize the way students and faculty members access and utilize the university's indoor badminton courts.

Our mission is to provide a seamless platform for booking badminton courts, promoting wellness, and enhancing the university experience for students and faculty alike

With PU-COURTMATE, booking a badminton court is as easy as a few clicks. Check real-time availability, select your preferred time slot, and confirm your booking—all from your computer or mobile device.

Join us in embracing the joy of badminton at Pondicherry University. Sign up, book your games, and experience the convenience of PU-COURTMATE today.
